How often should a hotel carpet be professionally cleaned?+
For high-traffic luxury hotels, we recommend professional deep cleaning every 3–6 months, with monthly spot treatment and preventive maintenance. Our AMC plans are designed around your specific usage and footfall.
Are your cleaning chemicals safe for hotel guests?+
Absolutely. All our chemicals are eco-safe, non-toxic, hypoallergenic, and biodegradable. They are safe for guests, children, and pets, and comply with environmental safety standards.
How long does it take for carpets to dry after cleaning?+
Using our advanced hot-water extraction and high-speed drying equipment, most carpets are dry within 2–4 hours. We schedule work during off-peak hours to minimize any disruption to hotel operations.
Do you offer emergency or same-day service?+
Yes. We offer priority emergency response for AMC clients and can arrange same-day or next-day service for urgent needs such as spills, stains before events, or post-event cleaning.
Can you handle large-scale properties across multiple locations?+
Yes. We work with hospitality groups and chains across Rajasthan and India. Our group AMC contracts offer coordinated scheduling, consistent quality standards, and centralized billing.
What is included in an Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)?+
AMC plans include scheduled deep cleaning visits, routine inspection, priority emergency response, spot treatment, detailed service reports after each visit, and a dedicated account manager for your property.
